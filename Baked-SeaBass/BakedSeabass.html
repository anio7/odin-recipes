Baked Sea-bass with Fried Plantain!!!

Home

this is one of my favorite dishes comprising of roasted fish and fried yummy plantains

Ingredients

  • 1 Sea-bass Fish
  • 1 Red Bell Peppers
  • 1 Red Scotch-bonnet
  • 1 Onion
  • 5 Clove of Garlic
  • Salt and Pepper
  • ginger
  • Badia

    • Steps:

    1. Wash and clean fish thoroughly, add lemon and set aside for 15mins.
    2. Using a food processor- add onion, garlic, red bell pepper, red scotch-bonnet pepper.
    3. Put a tablespoon of Avocado oil to simmer on the stove.
    4. Mix the processed Ingredients into the oil.
    5. Saute for 5mins.
    6. Set aside the mixture to cool.
    7. Paste mixture on and inside of fish.
    8. Cover fish with foil and place in over.
    9. Leave to cook for 15mins, then uncover.

    While the fish is baking...prepare the plantain:

    1. Slice the Plantain into pieces
    2. Put a pot on the stove with oil
    3. Place plantain in the oil to fry
    4. Allow plantain to become golden brown during the frying process
    5. When the plantain is cooked remove it from the oil and allow the oil to drain off.

    After the two are finished cooking place together on a plate and there you have it...voila!
